Privacy choices

Your data, your call.

Here's how to export your data, manage permissions, pause your account, or delete it permanently — all in a few taps.

1. Export your data

Open StallPass, sign in, and go to Profile. Tap Export my data and we'll build you a copy of your account — favorites, submissions, ratings — as a simple file you can save or share.

2. Pause your account

Need a break? Open Profile and tap Deactivate account. Your account is put on hold — nothing gets deleted — and you can reactivate anytime by signing back in.

3. Permanently delete your account

Open Profile and tap Delete account. Your personal data is removed right away. Community contributions (like ratings or bathroom additions) stay on the map anonymously so other people can keep finding the spots you helped verify.

4. Manage permissions and notifications

  • Turn location access on or off from your device settings at any time.
  • Pick which push notifications you want from the app's notification settings.
  • Signing out returns your phone to guest mode and stops syncing on that device.

5. What StallPass does — and doesn't — collect

StallPass only collects what it needs to show you nearby bathrooms and keep the map accurate. No paid ads, no rewarded-video tracking, no behavioral-analytics resell. If that ever changes, we'll update this page and the privacy policy before anything rolls out.

6. Still have questions?

Email support@stallpass.org and a real person will help you.
