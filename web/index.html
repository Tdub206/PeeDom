How StallPass works

One app helps people find bathrooms, and one dashboard helps businesses manage their listings.

The StallPass app helps people find nearby restrooms, save favorites, report issues, and keep using the app even when service is weak. The website keeps support, privacy, and terms pages live for customers, businesses, and app stores.

01

Find a restroom

People can search nearby bathrooms, filter results, view details, save favorites, report problems, and get directions.

02

Manage your business listing

Businesses can claim listings, update hours, confirm details, and manage promotions from a browser dashboard.

03

Keep things store-ready

Important pages like privacy, terms, and support stay live on the web so customers and app stores can always find them.

Business console

The business console is a real browser UI with persistent preferences, widget toggles, theme presets, setup scoring, notes, and support-link controls. It is a working control surface for day-to-day operations and a home for listing analytics.
