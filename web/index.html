How StallPass works

One app helps people find bathrooms, and one dashboard helps businesses manage their listings.

The StallPass app helps people find nearby restrooms, save favorites, report issues, and keep using the app even when service is weak. The website keeps support, privacy, and terms pages live for customers, businesses, and app stores.

Manage your business listing
