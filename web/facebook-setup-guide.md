# StallPass Facebook Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Facebook Business Page

### Basic Setup Instructions

1. **Go to Facebook Business Suite**
   - Navigate to `business.facebook.com`
   - Click "Create Account" or sign in with an existing Facebook account
   - Enter business name: **StallPass**

2. **Select Page Category**
   - Category: **App** or **Mobile App**
   - This helps Facebook correctly categorize and promote your page

3. **Choose Username**
   - Username: **@StallPass** (primary choice)
   - Alternative: **@StallPassApp**
   - Make it memorable and consistent across platforms

4. **Verify and Launch**
   - Complete the setup wizard
   - Add a profile photo and cover photo
   - Publish your page

## Step 4: Posting Strategy

### Frequency Recommendation
- **Launch Phase (Week 1)**: Daily posts
- **Growth Phase (Weeks 2-4)**: 3-4 posts per week
- **Steady State**: 2-3 posts per week

### Best Posting Times
- **Weekdays**: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-8 PM
- **Weekends**: 10-11 AM, 6-8 PM
- (Times can be adjusted based on your audience analytics)

## Step 5: Ongoing Engagement

### Daily Community Management
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
- Answer questions promptly
- Feature user reviews/ratings
- Share user success stories

### Content Mix
- 40% Educational (tips, how-to, features)
- 30% Community-focused (spotlights, testimonials, engagement)
- 20% Promotional (downloads, new features, business outreach)
- 10% Entertaining (memes, humor, relatable content)

### Monthly Tasks
- Review analytics in Facebook Insights
- Identify top-performing posts
- Adjust posting schedule based on engagement
- Plan content calendar for next month

## Step 6: Facebook Ads (Optional - Future Phase)

Once you have organic traction, consider:
- **App Install Campaigns**: Target iOS/Android users based on location, interests
- **Engagement Campaigns**: Boost high-performing organic posts
- **Traffic Campaigns**: Drive app downloads from existing audience
- **Lead Generation**: Capture business owner inquiries

Budget recommendation: Start with $5-10/day to test messaging.

## Troubleshooting Tips

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|

| Comments with complaints | Respond empathetically; use feedback to improve app |
| Business inquiries | Create a dedicated response; funnel to business portal |
| Algorithm changes | Monitor Facebook's Creator Studio announcements; adjust strategy |
