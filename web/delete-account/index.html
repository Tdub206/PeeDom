Delete account

Permanent account deletion is available inside the StallPass app.

Use this page as the public account-deletion URL for store metadata, reviewer notes, and privacy support.

1. Delete from the app

Open StallPass, go to the Profile tab, open the account controls, and choose the permanent deletion option. The app presents a dedicated deletion screen before the request is finalized.

2. What happens after deletion

  • Your personal account data is removed from the live account.
  • Active sessions are revoked and push-notification registrations tied to the account are cleared.
  • Anonymized community contributions may remain if needed to preserve map integrity and moderation history.

3. Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need privacy-related assistance, contact support@stallpass.app.
