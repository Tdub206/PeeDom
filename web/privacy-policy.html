StallPass Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026 · Last updated: April 5, 2026

StallPass ("we", "our", or "the app") is a crowdsourced bathroom-finder mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and an encrypted password, managed via Supabase Auth. Optional profile fields (display name, avatar) are stored only if you provide them.

Location data. With your permission, we use your device's approximate or precise location to show nearby bathrooms and sort search results. Location is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save a bathroom, submit a new listing, or report an issue at a location.

User-submitted content. Bathrooms you add, photos you upload, ratings, access codes you submit, reports, and favorites are stored in our Supabase database and associated with your account.

Device and diagnostic data. We collect crash reports and basic device information (OS version, app version, anonymized device identifier) to diagnose issues.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to operate the app, show you relevant bathrooms, sync favorites across devices, moderate user-submitted content, prevent abuse, and improve the product. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Permissions

StallPass requests the following Android permissions: location (to find nearby bathrooms), camera and photo library (only when you choose to attach a photo to a submission), and network access (to sync with our backend).

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the service providers required to run the app: Supabase (database, authentication, storage), Expo/EAS (build and update delivery), and Google Play Services (maps, crash reporting). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account from the profile screen at any time; this removes your profile and personally identifiable data from our systems. Anonymized, aggregated bathroom data you contributed may remain in the public database to preserve the usefulness of the crowdsourced map.

6. Children's Privacy

StallPass is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We use row-level security policies in our database, TLS for all network traffic, and industry-standard authentication practices. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us using the email below to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app or by email. Continued use of StallPass after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: support@stallpass.org
