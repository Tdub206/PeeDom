# StallPass Instagram Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Instagram Business Account

### Initial Setup

1. **Download Instagram** or go to instagram.com
2. **Create Account**
- Use email: support@stallpass.org
   - Create strong password
   - Username: **@stallpass** (primary choice)
   - Alternative: **@stallpassapp**
3. **Switch to Business Account**
   - Settings → Account type and tools
   - Select "Switch to professional account"
   - Category: **App** (or Apps & Games)
4. **Link to Facebook Business Page** (optional but recommended)
   - Settings → Accounts Center
   - This allows you to manage both from one place

## Step 6: Visual Style Guide

### Feed Aesthetic

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Blue #3459e6
- Secondary: Navy #203274
- Accent: Light background #f6f7fb
- Complementary: White, light grays

**Visual Style:**
- Mix of: Screenshot/mockups (40%), lifestyle photos (30%), memes/graphics (20%), user content (10%)
- Consistent filters/editing style across posts
- Modern, clean, approachable aesthetic
- Avoid overly dark or negative imagery

### Design Templates (Create 5-10 in Canva)

1. **Quote Posts**: White background, navy text, blue accents
2. **Tips/How-To**: Light blue background, icon-based layout
3. **Feature Highlights**: Screenshot with explanatory overlay
4. **Testimonials**: Photo frame with quote, brand colors
5. **Meme Format**: Relatable bathroom scenario format

### Font Recommendations

- **Headlines**: Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, Poppins)
- **Body Text**: Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto)
- **Captions**: Readable, not too stylized
- Avoid: Script fonts, decorative fonts that hurt readability

### Image Guidelines

- **Square Posts**: 1080x1080 pixels
- **Carousel**: 1080x1350 pixels
- **Reels**: 1080x1920 pixels (vertical video)
- **Stories**: 1080x1920 pixels
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High-resolution, no pixelation

## Step 8: Posting Schedule

### Recommended Frequency
- **Posts**: 3-4 times per week
- **Stories**: Daily (1-3 times)
- **Reels**: 2 times per week
- **Total Weekly**: 8-12 pieces of content

### Best Posting Times (Instagram Insights will refine this)
- **Weekday Posts**: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-7 PM
- **Weekend Posts**: 10-11 AM, 6-8 PM
- **Reels**: Post early morning (7 AM) or evening (6 PM)
- **Stories**: Throughout the day, spread content

### Content Mix Recommendation
- 30% Educational (tips, features, how-to)
- 25% Entertaining (memes, humor, reels)
- 25% Community (testimonials, spotlights, engagement)
- 20% Promotional (downloads, new features, CTAs)

## Step 11: Reel Strategy (Important for 2026 Growth)

### Why Reels Matter
- Instagram prioritizes Reels in the algorithm
- Higher reach potential than static posts
- Drives discovery to new audiences
- Strong engagement indicator

### Reel Content Ideas
1. **Educational Reels** (30%): How to use features, tips, tutorials
2. **Entertainment Reels** (40%): Funny scenarios, memes, relatable moments
3. **Behind-the-Scenes** (20%): Team, development, user stories
4. **Promotional** (10%): Feature launches, app updates

### Reel Best Practices
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds (check Instagram explore page)
- Add captions/text overlays
- Keep pace fast and engaging
- Include call-to-action
- Post at 7 AM or 6 PM for best reach

## Step 12: Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics to Track (Check Weekly)
- **Engagement Rate**: Comments + Likes / Followers
- **Reach**: Number of unique users who see your content
- **Impressions**: Total times content is displayed
- **Profile Visits**: Clicks to your profile from posts
- **Website Clicks**: Link in bio traffic
- **Follower Growth**: Weekly new followers

### What to Optimize
| If This Is Low | Try This |
|---|---|
| Engagement rate | More relatable/funny content, questions in captions |
| Reach | Use trending hashtags/sounds, post Reels more frequently |
| Profile visits | Improve call-to-action, add profile link to captions |
| Link clicks | More compelling CTAs, highlight download benefit |
| Follower growth | Engage with similar accounts, use community hashtags |

## Quick Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Low engagement | Post Reels 2x/week; use trending sounds; more questions in captions |
| Comments are negative | Respond professionally; ask what would help; show you care |
| Reach plateauing | Use new hashtag combinations; post at different times; try new content types |
| Not getting new followers | Engage with 10-15 similar accounts daily; comment authentically on their posts |
| High traffic but low conversions | Make link in bio clearer; add more download CTAs; test different link destinations |
