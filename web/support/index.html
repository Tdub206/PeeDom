Can users browse without an account?

Yes. People can use the main map and search features without an account. Signing in is only needed for features tied to an account, like favorites, submissions, moderation, and business claims.

How does account deletion work?

The app lets people deactivate or permanently delete their account from the profile page. Personal account data is removed, but anonymous community updates may stay so listings remain useful.

Where should stores and reviewers be sent?

Use this page as the public support link. Send reviewers to the privacy page for privacy details, the privacy choices page for export and privacy controls, the delete-account page for account removal, and the terms page for product rules.

How does the business dashboard surface analytics?

The business dashboard shows views, visitors, directions taps, ratings, favorites, and featured campaign status using real activity from StallPass.
