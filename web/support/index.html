Do I need an account to use StallPass?

No. Open the app and the map works right away. You only need to sign in if you want to save favorites, submit a listing, leave a rating, or claim your business.

Is StallPass free?

Yes. Finding bathrooms, saving favorites, and rating stops are all free. Business owners can optionally pay for featured placement on the map — that's how we keep the app free for everyone else.

How do I add a bathroom that isn't on the map?

Open the app, tap the add button, drop a pin, and tell us what's there — accessibility, baby-changing, customer-only, access code, anything helpful. A reviewer takes a look and the spot goes live as soon as it checks out.

Something's wrong with a listing. How do I report it?

Tap the location, then tap Report. Tell us if it's closed, dirty, inaccessible, or just plain wrong. Reports are handled quickly and the map updates automatically.

I own a business. How do I claim our listing?

Head to the business dashboard, sign in, search for your location, and submit a claim. Once approved, you can manage hours, amenities, coupons, and featured placement from one place.

How do coupons and offers work?

Businesses can publish coupons and specials that show up on their StallPass listing. Users redeem them in person — it's a simple way to turn a quick bathroom stop into a paying customer.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Profile, and choose Delete account. Your personal information is removed right away. Full steps live on the privacy choices page.

Still need help?

Email support@stallpass.org and a real person will get back to you. Include your device and the city you're in so we can help faster.
