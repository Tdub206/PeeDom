Privacy policy

How StallPass handles account, location, contribution, and support data.

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Scope and service operator

This Privacy Policy applies to the StallPass mobile app, the StallPass website, and related support surfaces operated under the StallPass brand. StallPass helps users discover bathrooms, submit community updates, manage saved locations, and for approved businesses, manage listing information and claim workflows.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information such as display name, email address, account status, and authentication records.
  • Location information when you grant location access so nearby search, emergency discovery, and route-aware features can function.
  • User-submitted content such as bathroom submissions, codes, photos, ratings, reports, accessibility updates, business claims, and related moderation records.
  • Device and app information such as push notification tokens, platform, app version, crash diagnostics, and reliability telemetry.
  • The current production mobile build does not enable a separate behavioral analytics ingestion endpoint.

3. How we use information

  • To provide search, discovery, map, routing, and bathroom detail features.
  • To operate accounts, favorites, contribution history, business tools, moderation, and support workflows.
  • To deliver notifications, fraud prevention, abuse detection, and service integrity checks.
  • To diagnose crashes, service regressions, and product reliability issues.
  • To honor export, deactivation, deletion, notification, and permission choices that users make.

4. How information is shared

StallPass does not sell personal information. We share information only with service providers and infrastructure partners that help us operate the product, such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, Sentry for crash monitoring, platform map providers such as Apple Maps on iOS and Google Maps on Android, Google Places for address and business lookup, and push-notification infrastructure required to deliver alerts. We may also disclose information when required by law, to enforce product rules, or to protect users, the public, or StallPass.

5. Ads, consent, and tracking

The April 5, 2026 production mobile build does not enable rewarded ads or a separate third-party behavioral analytics endpoint. If that changes in a future release, StallPass will update this policy, refresh the store privacy disclosures, and request any legally required consent before rollout. Users can also disable push notifications and other optional permissions from device settings.

6. Retention and deletion

StallPass retains account and operational data for as long as the account remains active or as long as needed to operate the service, investigate abuse, or satisfy legal obligations. The app exposes both deactivation and permanent account deletion flows. Personal account data is removed when deletion is completed, while anonymized community contributions may remain to preserve map integrity.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Export your data from the app.
  • Disable push notifications from within the app or system settings.
  • Deactivate the account if you want to stop using it without deleting data.
  • Permanently delete the account from the in-app account controls.
  • Use the public privacy choices page for instructions and support routing.
  • Request privacy support using the contact details below.

Public privacy controls and support instructions are also available on the privacy choices page.

8. Security

StallPass uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect data in transit and at rest. No service can guarantee absolute security, and you remain responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials and device.

9. Children's privacy

StallPass is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the service, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational practices. Material changes may be communicated through the app, the website, or other reasonable channels before or when they take effect.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion support, contact support@stallpass.app.
