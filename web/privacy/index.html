Privacy policy

How StallPass handles account, location, contribution, and support data.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the StallPass mobile app and the public StallPass website. StallPass helps users discover bathrooms, submit community updates, manage saved locations, and for approved businesses, maintain trusted listing information.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data such as display name, email address, and authentication state.
  • Location data when you allow location access so nearby bathrooms and emergency discovery can work.
  • User-generated content including bathroom submissions, codes, ratings, reports, accessibility notes, photos, and business claims.
  • Device and app diagnostics such as push tokens, platform, app version, and crash or reliability telemetry.
  • Operational analytics such as app bootstrap, authentication failures, and offline sync events.

3. How we use data

  • To show nearby bathrooms, search results, route-aware discovery, and live listing context.
  • To let users save favorites, contribute updates, and maintain account state across sessions.
  • To moderate community content and review business claims.
  • To deliver push notifications you have opted into.
  • To diagnose crashes, reliability issues, and operational regressions.
  • To support rewarded ad experiences where enabled and lawful.

4. Third-party processors

StallPass relies on service providers for infrastructure and app operations, including Supabase for authentication and data storage, Sentry for crash monitoring, Google Maps for map rendering, and Google AdMob for ad delivery where enabled. If a third-party SDK or processor touches data collected through the app, that practice must be reflected in store disclosure surfaces.

5. Ads, consent, and tracking

If StallPass serves ads, ad consent is requested where required. Store privacy disclosures must reflect any data collected or used by third-party advertising partners. Users can also disable push notifications and other optional permissions from device settings.

6. Retention and deletion

StallPass retains account and operational data for as long as the account remains active or as long as needed to operate the service, investigate abuse, or satisfy legal obligations. The app exposes both deactivation and permanent account deletion flows. Personal account data is removed when deletion is completed, while anonymized community contributions may remain to preserve map integrity.

7. Your controls

  • Export your data from the app.
  • Disable push notifications from within the app or system settings.
  • Deactivate the account if you want to stop using it without deleting data.
  • Permanently delete the account from the in-app account controls.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion support, contact privacy@stallpass.app.
