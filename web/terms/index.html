Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Eligibility

By using StallPass you represent that you can legally enter into these terms. If you create an account, you are responsible for maintaining its confidentiality and for activity that occurs under it.

2. User submissions

StallPass is powered by community and business contributions. If you submit listings, codes, ratings, accessibility details, reports, photos, or claim evidence, you represent that the information is lawful, reasonably accurate, and safe to publish.

3. Prohibited behavior

  • Submitting knowingly false or misleading bathroom information.

  • Attempting to manipulate rankings, points, reviews, or business claim outcomes.
  • Interfering with app security, rate limits, access controls, or moderation systems.

4. Business claims

Business users may submit ownership claims for locations. Claims may be approved, rejected, or revoked. Approval does not grant ownership of StallPass intellectual property. It grants operational control over listing details and any business tools made available for that listing.

5. Moderation and enforcement

StallPass may remove content, suspend accounts, or restrict access to preserve trust, safety, and data quality. Moderation decisions may consider abuse patterns, repeated false submissions, or business misrepresentation.

6. Availability and warranties

StallPass is provided as is. Bathroom hours, cleanliness, access policies, and live status can change. We do not guarantee that every listing is accurate at every moment, and users should still exercise judgment before relying on location details.

7. Account controls

StallPass supports account deactivation and permanent account deletion in-app. Some anonymized community contributions may remain after deletion to preserve public data integrity.

8. Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to support@stallpass.app.
