# How to delete your StallPass account and data

**Last updated:** April 14, 2026
**Host at:** https://stallpass.app/delete-account
**Contact:** support@stallpass.app

Google Play requires that we tell you exactly how to delete your account and data, even without opening the app. Here it is.

---

## Fastest: inside the app

1. Open StallPass.
2. Tap the **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **Settings**.
4. Tap **Delete my account**.
5. Confirm.

The account closes immediately. Your email, push subscriptions, favorites, and saved searches are removed within 30 days, including from backups.

---

## If you can’t open the app

Email **support@stallpass.app** from the address associated with the account. Put **“Delete my account”** in the subject line. We will:

1. Confirm the request from your email within 3 business days.
2. Delete your account within 30 days.
3. Email you a confirmation when deletion is complete.

We do not require any ID verification for deletion. The only thing we require is that the request come from the email on file.

---

## What gets deleted

- Your email address
- Your password hash
- Your display name
- Your push notification tokens and preferences
- Your favorites list and saved searches
- Your account-level settings

## What is anonymized (not deleted), and why

Submissions you contributed to the public map — bathrooms, codes, ratings, reports — remain on the map after deletion, but your name is disassociated. This keeps the community data useful without tying it to you. If you want a specific submission removed entirely, tell us which ones in your deletion email and we will remove them.

## What we keep, and why

- **Operational logs** (IP, request metadata) for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention.
- **Aggregate analytics counts** (e.g., “sessions per day”) indefinitely, because they do not identify you after aggregation.
- **Backups** for up to 30 days. Within 30 days of deletion, backup copies are overwritten.

## What we do not sell

We do not and have never sold personal information. Deleting your account does not require us to claw anything back from a data broker, because we never sent anything to one.

---

## Residents of California, the EU, UK, and other jurisdictions with specific data rights

You have the same deletion path as everyone else. You also have rights to access and port your data. Email privacy@stallpass.app and we will export or remove whatever you ask within 30 days (extendable once by 60 days for complex requests, per GDPR Article 12).
